# Guía de Contribución

## Repos
- app/ (React Native/Expo)
- functions/ (Cloud Functions)
- infra/ (scripts, reglas)
- docs/ (especificaciones, decisiones)

## Ramas
- main (estable), develop (último integrado), feature/*, hotfix/*

## Commits
- Convención tipo Conventional Commits (feat:, fix:, docs:, chore:, refactor:, test:)
- PR: plantilla con descripción, screenshots (si aplica), checklist (tests, lint, docs)

## Hooks
- Husky pre-commit: lint, tests rápidos.
- CI: build + tests + SCA.
